# Set of questions for Stakeholder

## Context Setting
- Why do we want to do this?
- Is the company looking for an ideal word/slide count? What is the ideal that we're looking for? Do we have any assumptions for reading time?
- In what time frame ws this data collected?
- Why did we run this expirement and what was the hypothesis we started with?
- Did you purchase this data? How did you collect this data?
- Are these findings an accurate representation of the groups involved? alternatively: Are there are bias we need to be aware of?

## Goal Alignment
- Which KPI's are important to you, which are you responsible for, what does it look like when you achieve beyond your kpis
